About Octopus Insurance LLC

Your Trusted Insurance Partner in Texas

Octopus Insurance LLC is an independent insurance agency dedicated to helping individuals and businesses find the perfect coverage at the best possible rates. Since 2011, we've been serving the greater Texas area with personalized insurance solutions.

As an independent agency, we work with over 25 top-rated insurance carriers to find you the coverage that fits your needs and budget. We're not tied to any one company, which means we can truly shop around to find you the best deal.

Our team of experienced agents understands that insurance can be confusing. That's why we're committed to explaining your options in plain English and helping you make informed decisions about your coverage.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional insurance services that protect what matters most to our clients while delivering outstanding customer service and competitive pricing.
